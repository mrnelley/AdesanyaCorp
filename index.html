Get To Know Us.

DESADE has over 25 years of experience specializing in healthcare operations and land development consulting. We create optimized teams comprised of industry experts matched to each project’s specific challenges and opportunities. We deliver results driven solutions that add value to business objectives.

Mission

DESADE’s mandate is to bring value based integrated solutions to the businesses and agencies with whom that we engage.

Enitan Adesanya
Chief Executive Officer
Enitan Adesanya has more than 25 years of experience in healthcare operations risk and financial management, and capital program due diligence, project risk management and cost controls. Enitan served as an executive in many financial operations and risk management roles at Kaiser Permanente for 25 years. As a risk management senior executive, Enitan was responsible for managing the strategic, operational, and regulatory risks associated with Kaiser’s $14 billion annual spend for land acquisitions and facilities development and operations; medical device technology acquisition and operations; strategic procurement and supply chain operations; and physical access and security services. Enitan earned a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration (Finance) from Ahmadu Bello University in Nigeria and an MBA from Golden Gate University in San Francisco. He also earned an Executive Leadership Certificate from the Harvard Business School. Enitan is a State of California Certified Public Accountant, is certified in Financial Forensics, and is Lean Six Sigma – Green Belt certified.

Ineda Adesanya
Principal
Ineda is a seasoned land use, capital development and construction management executive having worked in the land development industry for over 25 years. She has managed and directed a myriad of projects and programs including all land use types. In this capacity she has had to facilitate dialogue and manage relations between multiple stakeholders including owners, agencies, contractors, consultants, and community members. Ineda founded IPA Planning Solutions, Inc. in 2000 and managed the company as Principal and Chief Executive Officer. For 18 years, IPA provided land use, facilities, and construction planning and management services for governments, institutions and businesses. Ineda has served as the executive responsible for Peralta Community College District’s Physical Plant and $152 million capital program, and directed the Oakland Unified Schools District’s Facilities, Real Property and $400 million four-year capital program. Ineda is a graduate of the University of California at Berkeley with a Bachelor's degree in Architecture. She completed extensive study in City and Regional Planning and holds professional certification in both Facilities Management and Construction Management.
